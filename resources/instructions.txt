****** EAX-300/EAX-500 Testing Software *******

Equipment Needed:
	1) T-683 Test Base
	2) EAX-500 Test Head
	3) Assembled 102605 PWA

Setup instructions:
	1) Head unit is connected and secured to the test fixture.
	2) PWA is clamped down on the head unit.
	3) Test fixture is powered and the power LED is ON.
	4) Test fixture is connected to the computer.
	5) All slide switches are in the OFF position.
	6) Battery clip is properly clamped on the test head.

Press the "Program" button to program the board.
If no errors occur from programming, press the "Test" button to begin testing.
